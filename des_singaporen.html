singapo人

overview

In this project, we had to create a poster for a cultural exhibition about the Chinese citizens, using either Adobe Photoshop or Illustrator. In this case, I opted for Illustrator as that was the software I was best at using.

process


As the poster was about the Chinese Singaporeans, I wanted to display elements of their culture. In the moodboard, we can see images of delicious Chines cuisines, Chinese temples, lanterns and dragons. I also decided to use red and gold as they are colours strong linked to the Chinese.

In my sketches, I wanted to include an illustration as a means to capture the audience's attention. It would also immediately tell them what the poster is about. I had to consider the time I was given, so I stuck with a simple poster.

Originally, the dragon in the poster was coloured in. But I soon find out that it made the poster overwhelming as it fights for attention against the temple. So I instead opted to give it a gold outline design instead.

takeaways

From this assignment, I learnt the importance of colours and how it can make or break the design.
