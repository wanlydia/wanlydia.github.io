For this project, we needed to create

process


During the time of this assignment,
I was a fan of the Thirteenth Doctor from Doctor Who.
So I decided to create a set based on her.

The Thirteenth Doctor has this whimsical personality
where she's amazed by the stars but has a retro style.
Due to that, I wanted a retro-like letters
and add a small design like stars to each letter.

takeaways

From this assignment,
I get the opportunity to explore
all the various typography to create my very own.

A challenge I faced was ensuring
that all the letters have a consistent style.
