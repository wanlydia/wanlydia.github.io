overview

This is the first time we used Adobe XD. So as practice, our teacher told us to create a business website. I decided to fulfil my idea of a book cafe where there is an art class upstairs.

process


I began the process with a moodboard to encapsulate the mood I was aiming for - and that was cosiness. I chose brown as the main colour as it often means 'down-to-earth' or 'stability'. Plus the fact that it is a cafe, brown seemed the right colour to choose. The brown is contrasted with a light beige. I chose a serif font for this website as it looked professional but in the victorian era manner.
