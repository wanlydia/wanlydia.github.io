logo

overview

In this class,
we needed to create a branding for ourselves.
And one way to do that is by creating a logo.

process


If this was a branding for ourselves,
then I wanted this logo to encapsulate myself in my mind.

In my moodboard, I added things that I enjoy doing or
my favourite things like drawing or fantasy stories.
I ended up using a witch motif to show that I enjoy
all things magical and fantasy-related.

In the sketch,
I explored the witch motif as I like it
I looked at all the different ways I could use it
such as using the initial of my name
I liked the sketch where there is a mini me
on a book, with it opening up to a whole new world.
I found that it spoke to me and decided to use it as the foundation.

takeaways

From this project,
I ended up learning the process of exploring concepts.
