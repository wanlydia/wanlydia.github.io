overview

For this project, we needed to make a poster
in an Adobe software for a cultural exhibition

process


As the poster was about the Chinese Singapore culture,
I opted to display elements from that.
For example, the delicious Chinese food,
or the magnificent Chinese dragon.

I found that I am more confident in using Adobe Illustrator,
So I decided to illustrate the elements on the poster.

Originally, I wanted to make the dragon green
but I soon found that it makes the poster overwhelming
and clashes with the red and gold colour palette.
So I ended up giving it a gold outline instead.

takeaways

From this assignment,
I learnt the importance of the colours I used
and got to practice using Adobe Illustrator.
