About Me

Hi! I'm Lydia

I am a student from Republic Polytechnic, studying under the Diploma in Digital Design & Development.

Often described as optimistic, I strive to work towards my goals despite the circumstances. I am always seeking for improvements to challenge myself to build my skills and character.

What Do I Do?

Currently, I am learning to develop as well as design apps and websites. Traversing through this unfamiliar industry is daunting, but also exciting. I learn to code - something I never thought I could do, and find that it's not that bad.

Outside of that, I am a creative individual who spends their spare time making illustrations and trying out other forms of art such as watercolour painting and crocheting. I also help run the school's art club, further showing my passion in the arts.

What Do I Want?

I want to explore where this path will take me. I didn't expect to understand what was taught and I wish to learn more and expand my skills with this.
