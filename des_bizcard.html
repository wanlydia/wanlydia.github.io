business card

overview

Using the Adobe software,
we needed to make a business card for ourselves

process

I had created a logo prior to this project
and thought that it was suitable. So what I ended up doing is
make a business card that is based on the logo's design

As seen by the concept sketches
I was playing around with the logo's positions around the card
as well as the text placement.
I personally like the part where the tip of the castle
is appearing at the bottom of the card and thought that
it seemed suitable as the "cover"

takeaways

From this assignment,
I was able to practice experimenting the different layouts
and learnt that this is why we should do concept sketching.
